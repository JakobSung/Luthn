# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Participation in Luthn's issue tracker and other community spaces must remain
respectful, constructive, and focused on the project.

Examples of acceptable behavior include:

- giving actionable technical feedback;
- respecting different experience levels and viewpoints;
- removing private or sensitive data from reports;
- accepting maintainer decisions about project scope and contribution timing.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, publishing
another person's private information, spam, or repeated disruption of project
spaces.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, lock, or remove content and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when behavior violates these standards.

For confidential conduct reports, contact the repository owner through the
contact methods listed on the [JakobSung GitHub profile](https://github.com/JakobSung).
Do not include credentials, customer data, or unrelated private records.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, and other
official Luthn community spaces.
